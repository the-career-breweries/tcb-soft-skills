# M.Com Semester 2: Meetings & Leadership

Finance leaders chair some of the most critical meetings in a corporation: Audit Committees, Budget Reviews, and Earnings Calls. 

---

## 1. Leading by the Numbers

When chairing a financial review, the numbers dictate the agenda.
*   **The Pre-read:** Never reveal complex financial tables for the first time *during* the meeting. Send the numbers 48 hours in advance so the meeting can focus on *decisions*, not *reading*.
*   **The Clock:** Financial debates can rabbit-hole into microscopic details. As the chair, you must pull the conversation back up to the macro level.
