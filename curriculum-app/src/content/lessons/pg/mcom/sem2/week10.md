## 1. The Behavioral Interview (STAR Method)

Technical skills get you the interview; behavioral skills get you the job.
Structure every answer using the STAR method:
*   **S - Situation:** "During the year-end audit at Company X..."
*   **T - Task:** "I was tasked with reconciling a $2M discrepancy in inventory..."
*   **A - Action:** "I built a macro to cross-reference purchase orders against warehouse logs..."
*   **R - Result:** "I identified a systematic double-counting error, saving the client from a major compliance penalty."

### The STAR Flow in Finance
```mermaid
flowchart LR
    A[The Financial Problem] --> B[Your Specific Responsibility]
    B --> C[Your Analytical Action]
```
