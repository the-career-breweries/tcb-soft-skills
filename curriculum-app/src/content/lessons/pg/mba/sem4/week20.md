## From Student to Alumni

Your relationship with this institution does not end today; it evolves.

1. **The Alumni Network:** You are now part of a global, elite network of professionals. Use it, and more importantly, contribute to it.
2. **Mentorship:** Find a mentor for your first 3 years, and become a mentor to someone starting out.
3. **Continuous Learning:** The market shifts every 18 months. If you stop learning today, you will be obsolete in two years.

---

## The Personal Advisory Board

![Teamwork](/assets/teamwork.jpg)

Do not operate in isolation. You need a personal advisory board:
*   **The Challenger:** Someone who ruthlessly questions your decisions.
*   **The Cheerleader:** Someone who believes in you when you face massive setbacks.
*   **The Connector:** Someone who opens doors you didn't know existed.
*   **The Industry Veteran:** Someone who has already achieved your 10-year goal.

Build this board within your first year.

## Final Deliverable: The Letter

![Communication](/assets/communication.jpg)

Write a physical letter to yourself, to be opened exactly 5 years from today.
Include:
1. What you hope to have achieved.
2. The core values you promise never to compromise.
3. A reminder of the hunger you feel right now.

Seal it. Submit it to the facilitator. We will mail it to you in 5 years.
