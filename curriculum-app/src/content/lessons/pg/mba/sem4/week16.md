## The "Shark Tank" Protocol

1.  **Group up by Track:** Corporate, Entrepreneurial, or Consultant.
2.  **The Pitch (3 minutes):** Present your Day 1 findings (target companies, lean canvas, or RCA framework).
3.  **The Grill (5 minutes):** Your peers and the facilitator will brutally stress-test your assumptions.
4.  **The Pivot:** Take the feedback, adjust your plan, and prepare for execution.

---

## Why Peer Review?

![Teamwork](/assets/teamwork.jpg)

In the real world, you rarely execute in a vacuum.
*   **Corporate:** You must defend your strategies to stakeholders.
*   **Entrepreneurial:** You must pitch to skeptical investors.
*   **Consultant:** You must convince resistant clients.

Learning to take feedback objectively is a core executive trait.
