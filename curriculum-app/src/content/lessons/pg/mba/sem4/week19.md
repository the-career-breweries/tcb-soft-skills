## The Ultimate Presentation

Today is your Capstone Defence. 
You will present your comprehensive, final strategy to a panel of faculty, industry experts, and peers.

You are expected to integrate:
1. **Soft Skills:** Executive presence, body language, and active listening.
2. **Digital Fluency:** Proper utilization of MarTech, FinTech, and Data Analytics.
3. **Executive Process:** Clear scoping, financial viability, and crisis mitigation.

## Evaluation Rubric

![Feedback](/assets/feedback.jpg)

The panel will not be evaluating you as a student. They will evaluate you as a professional peer.

**Criteria:**
*   **Feasibility (30%):** Can this actually be executed in the real world?
*   **Clarity (30%):** Was the pitch concise, compelling, and well-structured?
*   **Resilience (40%):** How well do you defend your strategy during the Q&A cross-examination?
